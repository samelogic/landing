Emotional Pulse™ gives you a snapshot of what emotions your customers are currently experiencing.

As customers submit Snaps, Samelogic analyzes these videos, frame by frame, to understand what emotions are being expressed, what customers saying. The results are then aggregated into this view. Users, when clicking on these results, can dig deeper to hear what customers are saying.

Emotional Timeline pinpoints areas in a Snap key emotions are being expressed.

By keyframing the points where certain emotions are being expressed, companies can understand why customers are experiencing certain emotions. This is akin to having the customer, telling a story of their experience, right in front of you.

Track Emotional Changes with Customer Journey.

With Samelogic Customer Journey you can track the emotions individual customers experience over time. This enables a customer to be treated like they are the only customer the company has.
