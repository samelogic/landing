Mobile Video Surveys

Submit Mobile Video feedback by giving respondents a platform to share their stories from their mobile devices. People sharing their experiences through mobile videos has been one of the main reasons Instagram and Snapchat has experienced significant growth over the last few years. Seeing that this is now a key way to communicate, it is only necessary for us to use this as the new medium to collect survey feedback.

Emotional Correlation Radar

The emotional value of what is expressed facially and vocally then correlates both channels. The overlap is then correlated. If both facial and vocal emotion ques closely overlap, then we can safely assume respondents are being truthful. If not, we would advise taking a closer look.

Video Survey Review

When viewing video survey submissions, the scroll bar shows where particular emotions are expressed in the video and also, that is said is transscribed and analyzed. This data is then presented in an user friendly manner to for viewers to make actionable decisions.
